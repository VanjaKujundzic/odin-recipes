Carbonara recipe

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that originated in Rome.
It typically consists of pasta, eggs, cheese (usually Pecorino Romano), guanciale (cured pork jowl), and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Pecorino cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and add the pasta
  2. While the pasta is cooking crack the eggs and add Pecorino
  3. Cook the Pancetta in a large skillet with a small amount of olive oil
  4. Once the pasta is cooked drain it and mix it with Pancetta
  5. Final step is to add eggs and mix the pasta until it coats the Spaghetti
