Amatriciana recipe

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that originated in the town of Amatrice.
The sauce is primarily made from tomatoes, guanciale, pecorino romano and red pepper flakes. It has a rich flavor served with long pasta.

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Spaghetti
  • Canned tomatoes

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and add the pasta
  2. Cook guanciale and add canned tomatoes and red pepper flakes
  3. mix the guanciale and pecorino romano cheese
  4. Once the pasta is cooked drain it and mix it with the sauce
  5. Serve the pasta hot garnished with additional Pecorino romano cheese
