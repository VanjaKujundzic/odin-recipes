Bolognese recipe

Description

Bolognese sauce also knows as ragu alla bolognese comes from the city of Bologna. It's a hearty , meat based sauce made with ground meat.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Spaghetti
  • canned tomatoes

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and add the pasta
  2. While the pasta is cook the ground meat
  3. After cooking the meat add water, canned tomatoes and pepper
  4. Let it simmer for a bit, drain the pasta
  5. Combine the cooked pasta to the Bolognese sauce and mix it well
